Membership

Join our association and be part of our community!

Membership Benefits

  • Access to exclusive events and networking opportunities.
  • Support during challenging times, including the loss of biological parents, a child, or a partner.
  • Participation in community-driven projects and initiatives.

Membership Procedures

  • Must be 18 years of age or older.
  • Pay a membership fee of UGX 25,000.
  • Pay an annual subscription of UGX 30,000.
  • Must be a Ugandan citizen.
