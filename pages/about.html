Our Mission

To reduce on poverty and high un-employment rates among group members

Our Vision

To be a leading institution recognized for innovation and excellence in education, creating a world where every individual has the chance to thrive.

OBJECTIVES OF THE ORGANIZATION

THE ASSOCIATION emphasize on the following objectives

  • To promote Savings, credit scheme, and economic empowerment programs amongst the residents.
  • To promote unity among the members.
  • To improve in agricultural production for sustainable food in communities.
  • To promote health and sanitation in our community through WASH programs.
  • To advocate for human rights in our communities.
  • To start income generating activities among members.
