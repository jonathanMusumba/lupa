About Luuka Patriots Association

The Luuka Patriots Association is committed to reducing poverty and unemployment in Luuka Town Council by empowering community members through sustainable development initiatives.

Learn More About Us

Our Objectives

Poverty Reduction

We focus on initiatives aimed at reducing poverty by creating employment opportunities and empowering the youth.

Community Empowerment

We provide training and resources to help community members develop sustainable livelihoods.

Health and Education

Our programs aim to improve access to healthcare and education in the community.
